The Shire where it starts

Difficulty-Beginner

The Shire is the best beginnerâ€™s trip. It starts out slower and easier for those who are learning the ropes. Near the end you will hit some white rapids to provide an exciting ending. Since this is a beginner run, it will only cost $1000. The boats will be included but food will need to be provided by the customer. This is a two-day trip of off and on camping and rafting. It will inspire the adventurer to go for more white-water rafting adventures in the future.

The next river is Gondor

Difficulty-Advance

Gondor is for adventurers looking for more excitement in a longer trip. This will have more rapids to encounter than the Shire and it goes through scenic canyons an offers glimpses of wildlife. This trip is three days long and camping equipment will be provided by the customer. The food for day one will be provided by the company, but the food for the remainder of the trip needs to be supplied by the customer. The cost for this four-day trip is $1300. This is a great way to spend summer vacation. We hope you choose this option.

One does not simply raft into Mordor

Difficulty-Adventure

Mordor is the most challenging river trip. This requires at least two years of experience with whitewater rafting. In addition, adventurers on this trip should attend a white-water rafting safety seminar the morning the trip begins. This is a true adventure with perils and thrills not to be missed, lasting a full seven days. Camping gear will be provided by the customer. Boat rentals and food will be split between the company and the customer. This trip is our most advanced it will cost $1800. This is a once in a lifetime event for all who choose to undertake it. This will also be a test of skill on the river and your love the adventure.
