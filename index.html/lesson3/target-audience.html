The main targets for this website

The target audience for this website families that are planning a vacation or just want to do some outside chores. the other side of the audience are concerned residents about the weather in how severe it's going to be and if they should prepare for the worst. Also event coordinators would use this website as well like sports coaches and Boy Scout leaders.

1st Persona

Harry Stone

  • Age-32
  • Occupation- new dad with 3 kids
  • Why the user is here-Harry is planning play picnic at Springville reservoir and he wanted to make sure that it will not be too windy and cold so his kids can have a fun time and he can relax with his wife.
  • Site Access-laptop and google pixel

2nd Persona

Gale Storm

  • Age-43
  • Occupation- High School Teacher
  • Why the user is here- There is a lot of snow on the ground and there's more to come in she wants to know if school is still open and if it's safe for her to drive to the school.
  • Site Access- Iphone and desktop

Scenario that can go down

  1. How cold is in Mapleton?
  2. I cannot believe it is 100 degrees outside let me check.
  3. This week the scouts are planning a campfire cookout I need to make sure it's not going to rain that day and in the night.
  4. Do we need to cancel the activities because of Thunderstorms?
  5. Does it really rain cats or dogs.
